Read our story

Meet the three founders behind Zero Buzz: Alex, Mark, and Mikkel. As lifelong friends and beer enthusiasts, we shared a common passion for creating an alternative to traditional beer that still delivered the same enjoyable experience without the negative effects of alcohol.

Our journey began when we recognized a growing need for a non-alcoholic beer (NA Beer) that could provide the energy, courage, or relaxing feeling that people seek when enjoying a beer. We saw an opportunity to innovate in this space and set out to develop a product that could meet this demand.

Through extensive research and experimentation, we discovered a unique combination of ingredients that could replicate the desired effects of beer without the presence of alcohol. It was through this process that we stumbled upon the powerful trio of L-theanine, known for its calming properties, combined with the adaptogenic herb ashwagandha, offered a solution for relaxation and stress relief. Vitamin B1 provided an extra layer of support for overall well-being. With this innovative formula in hand, we created Zero Buzz.
